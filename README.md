## Currency converter
The first currency calculator I made allows you to convert the amount in PLN into one of six currencies:
 1. USD
 2. EURO
 3. CHF
 4. GBP
 5. CZK
 6. RMB
 
 ### Exchange rates come from the website nbp.pl of 04.04.2023 (Table No. 066/A/NBP/2023).
